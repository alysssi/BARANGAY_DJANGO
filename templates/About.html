About Us

The Barangay Information System (BIS) project is designed to bring a modern and efficient approach to the way our barangay operates. Our goal is to introduce an integrated digital platform that will simplify and streamline various aspects of our community's functions. With this system, we aim to improve communication, data management, and decision-making processes, ultimately benefiting the entire community. By embracing this advanced technology, our intention is to establish a more efficient, transparent, and citizen-focused administration within our barangay. This project will enable us to serve our residents better, ensuring that their needs and concerns are met promptly and effectively.
